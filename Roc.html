How It Works

Left Plot: Shows the distribution of prediction scores for negative class (orange) and positive class (teal). The vertical line is your classification threshold.

  • Area to the RIGHT of threshold: Instances classified as POSITIVE
  • Teal area right of threshold: True Positives (correctly identified positives)
  • Orange area right of threshold: False Positives (negatives incorrectly classified as positive)

Right Plot: The ROC curve shows all possible (FPR, TPR) pairs for different thresholds. The red dot shows the current threshold's position on the curve.

Try moving the threshold: Watch how the areas in the distribution change and how the point moves along the ROC curve!
